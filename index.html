Tic Tac Toe

About

Tic-tac-toe (American English), noughts and crosses (Commonwealth English), or Xs and Os (Irish English) is a paper-and-pencil game for two players who take turns marking the spaces in a three-by-three grid with X or O.

Source: Wikipedia

Strategies

Strategy 1

  1. Play your first X in a corner
  2. Try to win if your opponent plays the first O in the center.
  3. Win automatically if your opponent plays his first O in any square besides the center

Strategy 2

  1. Win with your fourth X
  2. Force a draw when the opponent starts in the center
  3. Try these out if your tic tac toe games always end in draws.

Strategy 3

  1. Symmetry is your friend
  2. Try to win if your opponent plays the first O in the center.
